## Domain: Music Streaming & Entertainment Analytics

This project belongs to the domain of digital entertainment analytics,
focusing on music streaming platforms. It combines consumer behavior
analysis with data-driven performance evaluation.

## Industry Context

Music streaming platforms generate massive data related to user
engagement, content popularity, and listening behavior. Analyzing this
data helps stakeholders understand market trends and optimize
strategies.

## Stakeholders

-   Music streaming platforms (Spotify, YouTube)
-   Artists and musicians
-   Record labels and producers
-   Marketing and analytics teams

## Business Goals

-   Identify high-performing artists and tracks
-   Compare engagement across streaming platforms
-   Understand listener preferences
-   Improve promotional and release strategies

## Analytical Goals

-   Analyze streams, views, likes, and comments
-   Compare performance by album type
-   Evaluate the influence of audio features
-   Assess the impact of official videos
-   Perform platform-wise comparisons

## End Goal

The end goal of this project is to provide actionable insights that help
stakeholders: - Enhance audience engagement - Optimize music release
strategies - Make data-driven marketing decisions - Improve content
performance across platforms

## Real-World Applications

-   Artist performance benchmarking
-   Platform growth analysis
-   Music recommendation systems
-   Marketing campaign optimization
